Founder & Visionary

Alin Kipnis

“We didn't start DKF to write checks. We started it to build something that outlasts us — a system that heals, grows, and keeps giving.”

Co-founder of NextVision — one of Israel's most successful defense-tech companies, valued at over $5 billion and trusted by the IDF, Elbit, and Israel Aerospace Industries. Alongside her husband, Alin built a global leader in stabilized drone camera systems used by militaries worldwide. With deep roots in design, real estate, and business development, she brings to DKF the same entrepreneurial precision that turned a startup into a defense industry powerhouse — now channeled into healthcare impact.

The team

Leading with purpose

Head of Innovation & Ventures

Ira Fradkin

Co-founder of DUO Interior Design, where she has led the design of over 5,000 sqm of commercial and residential spaces. With expertise spanning space planning, sustainable materials, and project management, Ira brings an architect's precision to DKF's investment strategy — identifying Health-Tech ventures with the same eye for structure, function, and long-term value.

Head of Philanthropy & Social Impact

Karine Bar Or

Former VP of Civic Engagement at the Israeli American Council (IAC) and a veteran of the Posen Foundation — one of Israel's leading philanthropic organizations in education and Jewish culture. With a career spanning nonprofit leadership, national program management, and strategic partnerships across two continents, Karine brings deep expertise in building initiatives that scale. At DKF, she ensures every philanthropic dollar creates measurable, lasting impact.
