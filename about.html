Founder & Visionary

Alin Kipnis

“We didn't start DKF to write checks. We started it to build something that outlasts us — a system that heals, grows, and keeps giving.”

Co-founder of one of Israel's most valued defense-technology companies — a global leader in advanced imaging systems trusted by the IDF and major defense contractors worldwide. With decades of experience building ventures across defense, design, and real estate, Alin brings to DKF the strategic precision of someone who scaled a startup into a multi-billion-dollar enterprise — now channeled entirely into healthcare impact.

The team

Leading with purpose

Head of Innovation & Ventures

Ira Fradkin

With a degree in Biology and years of hands-on experience in clinical research at global pharmaceutical companies and leading hospitals, Ira understands healthcare from the inside. She holds a degree in Multidisciplinary Design — a unique combination that allows her to see both the science behind medical innovation and the human experience it serves. At DKF, she brings this dual perspective to every investment decision — identifying Health-Tech ventures with an eye for clinical value, scalable design, and real-world impact.

Head of Philanthropy & Social Impact

Carine Bar Or

With over a decade of experience in the philanthropic sector, Carine has held senior leadership roles at some of Israel's and America's most respected foundations and nonprofit organizations. Her career spans strategic grantmaking, national program management, community empowerment, and advocacy on two continents. More than a profession, philanthropy is her calling — Carine is driven by a deep love for giving and a belief that structured generosity can transform lives. At DKF, she ensures that every initiative is not just well-funded, but deeply meaningful.
