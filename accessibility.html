Accessibility Statement

Last updated: April 2026

Our Commitment / המחויבות שלנו

David Kipnis Foundation is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

קרן דוד קיפניס מחויבת להנגשת האתר לאנשים עם מוגבלויות. אנו פועלים לשפר באופן מתמיד את חוויית המשתמש ומיישמים את תקני הנגישות הרלוונטיים.

Standards / תקנים

This website strives to conform to the Israeli Standard SI 5568, which adopts the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 at Level AA, as required by the Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities Regulations (Accessibility Adjustments for Service), 5773-2013.

Measures Taken / צעדים שננקטו

Keyboard navigation: All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard.

Screen readers: Semantic HTML, ARIA labels, and alt text are used throughout.

Color contrast: Text meets WCAG AA contrast ratios.

Skip navigation: A skip-to-content link is available for keyboard users.

Accessibility widget: An on-page accessibility tool allows users to adjust font sizes, contrast, spacing, and more.

Known Limitations / מגבלות ידועות

Despite our best efforts, some content may not yet be fully accessible. We are actively working to identify and resolve any remaining barriers. If you encounter difficulties, please contact us.

Accessibility Coordinator / רכזת נגישות

For accessibility-related inquiries, feedback, or to report an issue, please contact us at: info@dkfound.com

לפניות בנושא נגישות, משוב, או לדיווח על בעיה, נא צרו קשר ב: info@dkfound.com
