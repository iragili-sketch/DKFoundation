Featured story

A clinic that changed a neighborhood

In 2025, DKF funded the renovation of a community clinic serving over 3,000 families in southern Israel. What was once a crumbling facility is now a modern, dignified space for primary care. The outcome: a 40% increase in preventive screenings and a community that finally feels seen.
